*    United States declares public health emergency over 'monkeypox' outbreak

* US declared a public health emergency on Thursday over monkeypox virus outbreak in the nation
* Over 7,100 Americans are affected with monkeypox virus in US
* The monkeypox virus spreads through prolonged skin-to-skin contact, including hugging, cuddling and kissing,
   as well as sharing bedding, towels and clothing
* But health officials emphasize that the virus can infect anyone.
* No one in the United States has died so far due to monkeypox
